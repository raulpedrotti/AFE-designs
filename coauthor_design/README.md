**[1]A Digital Block Control for A Photoplethysmography System in CMOS 65 nm**

Abstract: This paper proposes a digital block to control an LED driver, a programmable gain amplifier (PGA) and an IDAC for an analog photoplethysmography (PPG) analog front- end circuit. The paper shows the results of testbenches for the digital circuit as well as the logical and physical synthesis that can be verified for the LED driver circuit with an estimated power consumption of 73.211 nW and an estimated area of 291 μm2. The IDAC PGA digital circuit has an estimated power consumption of 122.32 nW and an estimated area of 559.840 μm2. For both circuits, this results in an estimated total power consumption of 195.531 nW and an estimated total silicon area of 850.84 μm2.
